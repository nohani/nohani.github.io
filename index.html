I am a web developer that has experience working with a range of technologies along the fullstack. I am mainly proficient in Ruby, Ruby on Rails, React, Redux, GraphQL, Javascript, HTML5 and CSS3. I have also used relational and non-relational databases, such as PostgreSQL and MongoDB, respectively. I was drawn to software engineering because of my affinity for problem solving and creativity. Although I initially studied architecture at Barnard College of Columbia University, making the transition was seamless because I was able to take the critical thinking skills I learned and apply it to web development. In my spare time, I enjoy thinking about design, singing, and playing piano.

Thought Bubble

Thought Bubble is a full-stack, single page, application that emulates the popular blogging site, Tumblr. To build this, I used React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, CSS3 and Webpack.

Brainchild

Inspired by a popular Kanban productivity application, Brainchild provides users with an interactive UI for their workflow. The main technologies used were React, MongoDB, Express, Node, GraphQL, Apollo GraphQL, and Docker.

Music Maestro

Music Maestro is a mobile-first, full stack app that allows users to search for artists, venues and musical events. The technologies used include MongoDB, Express, React, Node, Javascript and CSS3.

Dream

In Dream, one can play loops and synthesizers to create their own music. The technologies used were Javascript, CSS3, Tone.js and HTML5.
